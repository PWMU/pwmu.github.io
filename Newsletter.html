PWMU was founded in 1890 to encourage prayerful interest in, and support for, missionaries of the Presbyterian Church of Victoria (PCV).

PWMU membership is open to members and adherents of PCV congregations. Members are usually organised into branches operating under the authority of the Session of the local congregation.



Members receive regular news about missionaries supported and sponsored by the PCV via their monthly newsletter, produced 11 times a year (excludes January)


The PWMU Newsletter reports on

- news from missionaries, reprinted from their reports to the Council and regular prayer letters

- general news from mission organisations

- activities around the State to raise funds

- reports from Council and its various Conveners

- reports on general activities of Branches and Presbyterial Associations


PWMU also offers children within congregations the opportunity to support missions work via

- Dayspring Children's Newsletter

which brings some of the news in the main newsletter to the attention of younger readers in a bright, colourful format three times a year.
